I am an Iranian cinematographer, film director of photographer who has worked with a number of renowned Iranian directors such as Mohsen Makhmalbaf, Hossein Latifi, Dr Hadi Karimi and Manouchehr Hadi. I am one of the top nominees for the Los Angeles Global Festival Cinematographer for the movie Are you Volleyball.
